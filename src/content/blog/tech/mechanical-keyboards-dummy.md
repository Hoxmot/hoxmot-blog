Hall Effect switches have changed the competitive landscape. Being able to adjust your actuation point is a game-changer.

This summary explains the tech behind the click.

## Magnetic Sensors

Unlike traditional mechanical contacts, these use magnets to detect depth. This allows for features like "Rapid Trigger", where the key resets the moment you lift your finger.

- Faster response times
- Adjustable actuation
- Durability

Is it worth the price? For generic typing, maybe not. For CS2? Absolutely.
